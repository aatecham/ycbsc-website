About the Club

A welcoming club for social and competitive cue sports.

Our Story

Yarraville Club Billiards and Snooker Club brings together local players who enjoy quality tables, good company, and a respectful environment for the game.

We are a sub-club of the Yarraville Club and host multiple Australian and State championships each year.

With the largest membership of any snooker club in Australia, new players and experienced players are both welcome. Members support one another with practice, advice, and social events across the year.

Facilities

  • 8 full-size snooker tables and 2 pool tables
  • Snooker tables are mostly Alcocks
  • Includes one Professional Star table
  • Regular social play sessions
  • Friendly club competitions and events
  • Supportive member community

History

The Billiards and Snooker Club started soon after the Yarraville Club was formed on its current site at 135 Stephen Street, Yarraville.

After more than half a century, the Billiards and Snooker Club moved a short walk down to its current location. It has always remained a core part of the Yarraville Club's identity.

As part of the new location, the club added 2 additional full-size tables and 2 pool tables.
