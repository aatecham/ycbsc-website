Membership and Fees

Choose a membership type that suits you and join the club community.

Standard Member

$120 per year

Payable upfront. A one-off $50 joining fee applies.

Junior Member

$0 per year

Must be 18 years of age or under. No joining fee applies.

Regional Member

$40 per year

Must reside 150+ km from the club. No joining fee applies.

Member Benefits

  • Personal access FOB pass to venue
  • $40 in meal vouchers to Yarraville Club
  • Membership of Yarraville Club
  • 8 tables with new cloths every year
  • Participation in club events

How to Join

  1. Complete the membership form and choose your membership type.
  2. Provide proof of address or age if required.
  3. Pay upfront (to June 30).

Bank transfer: YCBSC BSB: 063-181 Account: 00901708 (use your full name as reference).
